2021 | 4-Month Project | 10 People Team
The bottom of the scorching pot will continuously cause damage to the players standing on it. The players can avoid their own contact with the bottom of the pot by stacking on top of other players, while using different items and interacting with the scene to achieve the purpose of stacking, thus avoiding the fate of losing their souls due to boiling
