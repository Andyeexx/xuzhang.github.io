Overview

2022 | 3-Week Project | 3 People Team
The enemies will come and throw javelins toward you, and your goal is to use a shield to collect those javelins and fight back.
Also, there would be projectiles like huge rock that you have to dodge. When the enemies are close, you have less time to react. So you need to kill them as soon as possible before they get close.
